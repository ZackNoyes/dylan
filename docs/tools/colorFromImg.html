Color From an Image

A tool by Dylan

Image Tools

Upload your image, then select "Select Image". After the image loads click on the part of the image that you want the color of. Then go over to the panel on the left. Click on the panel on the left to pick the color you want and it will display. To re-pick just exit the image and re-hover over it.
