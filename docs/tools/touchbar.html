Touch Bar App

An app for the Mac Touchbar by Dylan

Fun

The touch bar app randomly spams colors on your touchbar after clicking the "Party" button. You can stop the flashing colors by pressing the "Stop" button.
Please note, when you quit the app to fully quit it. Pressing the x button at the top of the window will close it, but you will have to quit and then re-open the app for it to run again!
