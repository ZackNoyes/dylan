About my application

I spent over 30 hours of hard work, bug fixing and getting no where to complete my application. Every spare minute of my weekends, afterschool and during I worked hard to complete the project.

Orientation!

First day at WWDC - Orientation!

Today was awesome! We started off by walking to the convention center where we recieved our badges, jackets and pins. We then bussed off to a secret location.


It turns out that the secret location was Apple Park! We got to go inside Tantau 9 and visit Cafe Mac's. After that we went inside Apple Park and into the Steve Jobs Theatre. Inside we got talks from 6 high ranking Apple people including Tim Cook!


Afterwards Tim Cook appeared and I was lucky enough to get a selfie with him!
