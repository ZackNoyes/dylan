Indicators Outline

Physicians per 1000 people

This is a measure of how many physicians there are per 1000 people in the country. With more physicians a country generally has better access to doctors and medical treatment which creates a healthier population. The more physicians also represents to some extent tertiary education which represents better wellbeing. When people are healthier, generally they live longer, can work longer and people are happier and this greatly increases wellbeing

Percentage of population with Electricity Access

This measures how many people in the country have access to electricity as a percentage of the total population. Generally the countries with better electricity access are move developed as they can provide better services for their citizens. Electricity is also a basic requirement of life and those with electricity access are generally happier as their lives are easier. Electricity Access is a measure of well being as it indicates the lifestyle of the population.

Population below poverty line

This measures how many people in the country are below the poverty line as set by The World Bank. This is represented as a percentage of the total population. This indicateds wellbeing by representing the lifestyle of a country.

Life Expectancy

This measures how long people are expected to live in a given country. Generally those who live longer have better health, education and lifestyle which results in better wellbeing.
